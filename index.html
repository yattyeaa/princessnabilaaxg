Written at 2am, meant for always

Things I carry
in silence

To you, always —

I want you to know that I noticed you before you ever noticed me. I watched you exist in a room once and felt something shift — oh, so this is what it feels like. Like recognizing something you have spent your whole life almost knowing.

You find beauty in things other people walk past. You make space for people without ever making noise about it. You are tender in the exact places the world usually makes people hard. That is rare. That is not a small thing.

There are nights I want to return to the very first time we spoke — just to feel that single second again. The one where something settled inside me, and some quiet part of me already knew: she is going to be everything.

I don't know how to measure what this is. But I know it is the most honest thing I have ever built. And I would build it again — every time, in every life, in every universe. Always, inevitably, back to you.

— yours, completely.
